oh no there is a shiny new SSG

July 11, 2021
  • site
  • ssg
  • astro

Saw card/Astro mentioned in my Twitter feed today. Tempting.

Been looking for a gateway SSG into JavaScript. Something with a little more built-in than card/Eleventy, but maybe a bit less than card/Gatsby. I think I’ll play with Astro — see if it keeps my attention.

Will it keep my attention long enough to replace the Hugo version? Precedent says “no.” But it should be fun just the same.

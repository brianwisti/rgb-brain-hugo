Tying Bits Together

March 4, 2010
  • coolnamehere
  • blogspot

I’m working on tying together the blog and my site. A little JQuery magic expands the card/RSS feed of the Blogger blog for visitors to coolnamehere. I’ll improve the integration over time, probably by adjusting the template used for the blog. Meanwhile, I have more incentive to use Blogger.

I’ll probably add feeds from other services to the site, such as Buzz or Twitter. I use those frequently, and they are great for the short thoughts that don’t belong in either a blog post or a static page.

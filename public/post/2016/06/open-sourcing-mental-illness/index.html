Open Sourcing Mental Illness

June 21, 2016
  • mental-health
  • marginalia

Watching YAPCNA videos, and the most important to me today had nothing to do with card/Perl. I loved Ed Finkler’s talk about mental health among developers. His OSMI project - Open Sourcing Mental Illness - aims to improve workplace and community awareness and resources for mental health. Good stuff. Many domains and communities need this effort, and his chosen community is tech. Worth checking out.

I already knew [card/Logseq](../../../card/Logseq.md) does rich paste with `C-v`. Today I learned plain-text paste is `C-V`. That's <kbd>Control</kbd> + <kbd>Shift</kbd> + <kbd>v</kbd>.

Makes it a lot easier to paste bookmark titles.
